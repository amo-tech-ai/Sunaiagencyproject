An AI-powered content creation platform that helps brands plan photo shoots and videos  . The AI analyzes the brand’s style guide, past content, and current market trends, then decides what to shoot and how creative it should be. Teams can control this using a **Creative Temperature** setting, from safe and conversion-focused to bold and trend-driven.

The platform generates platform-specific shot lists, photo and video ideas, captions, taglines, CTAs, and social ad concepts for Amazon, Instagram, and paid media. By planning everything in advance and reusing the best assets, brands get on-brand, modern content the first time, with fewer revisions, less guesswork, and faster execution.
