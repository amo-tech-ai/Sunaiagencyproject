- Padding: py-24
- Stack hero slightly earlier

- All single column
- Full-width buttons
- Reduced padding (py-16 to py-20)

## CONTENT TONE

### Headlines
- Direct, confident statements
- 3-7 words maximum
- Outcome-focused
- No hype or buzzwords

### Body Copy
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
- Active voice
- Specific, not vague
- Avoid: "cutting-edge", "revolutionary", "game-changing"

### CTAs
- Action verbs: "Start", "Book", "View", "Get"
- Clear outcome expected
- No generic "Learn More"
